I am define by my faith in Jesus Christ, which I invited in my life as Lord and Savior on I was 17 years old. My story with God start in my childhood when my grandparents describe Him as my creator. My Grandfather told me the story of Noah who was delegated by God to build an Ark, because the weaknesses of the people where determine God to destroy His Creation by Flood. So I discover that upset God is not smart and it has consequences that can make my life miserable. So all my childhood I try to please this God which was not so personal but more like a distance entity who has the power to punish me if I am not please Him. On the other hand my grandmother teach me how to pray and I did that with religious conscience. Then when I was 14 years old I become rebellious against God because I fell to go to a good high-school so I blame God because my failure. For 3 year I ignore God but when I discover He died for my sin to give me life I surrender to Him. After 26 year I have no regrets for that decision. My faith is what define me.
